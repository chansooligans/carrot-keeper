# The Whispering Roots — long arc

The Whispering Roots are a network of ancient magical roots beneath rabbit lands. For generations they kept wolves out of Lodi. They are weakening. Noddy is the new Carrot Keeper, inheriting the role from her grandmother Hazel, who inherited it from her own grandmother. Carrots — the right carrots, grown in the right soil — feed the roots.

The wolves are not pure villains. The original pact between rabbits and wolves was broken long ago, and the Roots are the scar tissue holding the world together. By the end, Noddy must decide whether to restore the old pact or rewrite it.

## Chapters

### Chapter 1 — A Quiet Day in Lodi *(shipped)*
Cozy intro. Festival errands. Lost baby bunny. Midnight raid. The Elder Carrot is stolen during the Harvest Festival blackout. Hazel hands Noddy her old satchel and says "It's starting again." Hook: tracks lead into the forest.

### Chapter 2 — Mossfoot Forest
First wolf encounters. Combat unlocks. Noddy meets a wounded fox kit named **Sage** who joins as a party member. They find the first weakened Whispering Root and learn the carrots feed it. A lost rabbit hermit named **Tobias** points the way deeper.

### Chapter 3 — The Sunken Burrow
Underground ruins of the old rabbit civilization. Lore reveal: rabbits were once a great underground people, the Roots their work. A second root node here is corrupted, not weakened — something darker is happening.

### Chapter 4 — The Howling Marsh
Meet rabbits who *negotiate* with wolves and live alongside them. Moral complexity: the Roots may be the wrong solution. A wolf elder named **Sable** offers Noddy the wolf side of the story.

### Chapter 5 — Moon Hill
Climax. The original pact site. Noddy chooses: restore the Roots, dissolve them, or weave a new pact that includes both peoples.

## Recurring motifs

- **Carrots** as life-force, not just food. Different carrots = different powers.
- **The bandana** Hazel gives Noddy was her own grandmother's — flashbacks possible.
- **Whispers** — the Roots literally whisper. Noddy starts hearing them more clearly each chapter.
- **Lodi shrine** — the weathered carrot shrine in the village is the closest Root-anchor. State of the shrine reflects Roots' health.

## Cast (recurring)

- **Noddy** — protagonist. Grey rabbit, red bandana.
- **Hazel** — grandmother, former Carrot Keeper. Stays in Lodi but appears in dreams + key moments.
- **Sage** — fox kit, party member from Ch. 2. Curious, brave, originally an outsider.
- **Tobias** — hermit rabbit, old friend of Hazel.
- **Sable** — wolf elder, antagonist-then-ally arc.
- **The Whisperer** — the entity behind the Roots. Identity revealed in Ch. 5.

## Design rules for future chapters

1. Each chapter restores or transforms one Root node.
2. Each chapter introduces exactly one new ally and one new threat type.
3. Combat scales but is never the focus — exploration and dialog drive it.
4. Carrots collected in earlier chapters carry over and matter (specific named carrots unlock things).
